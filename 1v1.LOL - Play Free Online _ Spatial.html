1v1.LOL

Lior Alterman

Advertisement

1v1.lol is a competitive online third-person shooter where you build your way around the map tactically. You can build platforms, ramps, and walls to aid your defenses and gain an advantage. The main game mode is 1v1 battle royale, where the winner is the last player standing.

How to Play

1v1.LOL features many game modes, including battle royale, 1v1, and box fight (2v2), and free for all. In most cases, you'll be playing battle royale so your objective is to eliminate enemies and be the last remaining survivor. Players can choose from a variety of weapons and build structures to protect themselves.

This game challenges players to not only outshoot but also outsmart their opponents through rapid building and strategic manipulation of the environment.

With its blend of shooting and construction mechanics, it beckons gamers who seek a test of reflexes, aiming accuracy, and the ability to think several steps ahead under pressure.

The key to dominance in 1v1.LOL lies in mastering the nuanced art of fast building, securing high ground, and perfecting shooting techniques, all while employing advanced defensive strategies and psychological warfare to outmaneuver opponents.

Tips & Tricks

Shoot and build

Mastering the art of swift building sets the accomplished players apart from novices in 1v1.LOL. Erecting barriers and ramps swiftly can shield you from enemy fire and give you a strategic height advantage. Practicing these skills in less pressured environments can enhance your speed and efficiency during crucial moments.

Observe your surroundings

Observation plays a pivotal role in securing victories. By watching replays of your matches or those of skilled players, insights into effective strategies and common pitfalls become apparent. This analytical approach can significantly improve decision-making and tactical planning.

Utilize high ground to your advantage

The tactic of securing high ground stands as a pivotal element in 1v1.LOL, profoundly shaping the dynamics of combat and strategy between rivals.

This advantageous position not only affords players a panoramic view of their battlefield but also places them in an assertive stance to steer the direction of engagement.

Two core strategies emerge paramount in leveraging this superior vantage point: mastering the ability to ascend to high ground swiftly, distinguishing the adept from the hesitant, and utilizing this elevation to dictate the tempo and outcome of the clash.

Game Modes

There are three core game modes in 1v1.LOL:

  • Battle Royale Duos (2v2 battle royale)
  • 1v1
  • Box Battle (2v2)

Along with the core modes, you can also play:

  • JustBuild (practice and improve building)
  • Aim Trainer (practice and improve aiming)
  • Party (join a specific private game or create a game for your friends)

Controls

  • WASD or arrow keys to move
  • Left mouse button to shoot/build
  • Spacebar to jump
  • Shift to crouch
  • E to use an item
  • R to rotate stair/reload
  • Z, X, C, V, or Y to switch building platforms
  • F, 1, or 2 to switch weapons
  • G to make door on platforms

FAQ

Is JustBuild.lol the same as 1v1.LOL?

No, JustBuild.LOL is the non-combat version of 1v1.LOL. It's just for building.

Is 1v1.LOL similar to Fortnite?

Yes, 1v1.LOL is similar to Fortnite but simple and played in your web browser.

Can I play 1v1.LOL on mobile?

1v1.LOL is now available on Android and Apple, as well as JustBuild.lol.

Can I play Battle Royale in 1v1.LOL?

There is a Battle Royale mode in 1v1.LOL which allows up to 10 players. There are other modes that let you play against more than 1 player too.

Can I play 1v1.LOL unblocked?

To play 1v1.LOL unblocked, ensure you’re accessing the game through legitimate sources. If you’re facing restrictions, you can try playing from your personal device or using a VPN to bypass any network blocks. Always prioritize safe and authorized ways to access the game.
